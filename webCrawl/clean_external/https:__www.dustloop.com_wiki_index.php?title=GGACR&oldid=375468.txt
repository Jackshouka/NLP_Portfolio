Released six years after the previous installment: Guilty Gear XX Accent Core, Accent Core Plus R contains a large amount of balance changes. Some characters have had minor tweaks while other characters have been completely reworked. Getting StartedIf you are new to +R, or Guilty Gear in general, then take a look at the Starter Guide and FAQ. If you play on Hitbox, keyboard, or any other leverless input device, consider reading the  Leverless Input Guide. If you need help using training mode, there is a guide for that. In order to select an EX character, highlight the character you wish to play on the Character Select Screen and press the Start button. For detailed information on how to unlock these characters, and the differences between them and standard characters, refer to this guide.
